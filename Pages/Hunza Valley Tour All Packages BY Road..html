Tour Plan

Departure from Islamabad for Naran 8:00 am. Travel through Hazara Motorway and Balakot. Stopover at Balakot near River Kunhar and Kawai Waterfall. Arrival in Naran and transfer to hotel. Photography, Sightseeing and free time in Naran.
Overnight stay in Naran

Breakfast. Departure for Skardu. Travel through Lulusar Lake and Babusar Pass. Arrival in Chilas and drive to Jaglot. Travel on the newly built Jaglot Skardu road with stopovers on the way. Arrival in Skardu and visit Shangrila Resort and Upper Kachura Lake (if time allows otherwise on lake day). Transfer to hotel.
Overnight stay at Skardu

Breakfast. Visit Manthoka Waterfall. Photography and Sightseeing. Departure for Shigar Valley. Stopover for photography at Cold Desert. Enjoy distance view scenic Blind Lake and Sarfaranga Desert. Visit old town Shigar and famous Shigar fort (built in 17th century by the Raja of Amacha Dynasty of Shigar). Leisure time in the peaceful lawn of Shigar fort. Visit Amburiq Mosque (one of the oldest in Baltistan). Drive back to Skardu.
Overnight stay at Skardu

Breakfast. Drive to Khaplu. Stopover at confluence point of river Shyok River and Indus. Sightseeing near-by waterfall. Drive to Khaplu. Visit old town of Khaplu and famous Khaplu fort (built in the mid-19th century). Visit 600 years old famous Chaqchan Mosque. Drive back to Skadu..
Overnight stay in Skardu

Breakfast. Departure for Deosai Plains. Visit Sadpara Lake on the way. Arrival in Malik Top and enjoy the scenery of majestic Deosai Plains. Continue drive in Deosai and visit Bara Pani. Drive till Sheosar Lake. Sightseeing in the surroundings and if weather is clear, witness mighty Nanga Parbat (9th highest mountain from distance). Drive back to Skardu till evening
Overnight stay at Skardu

Breakfast. Departure for Hunza Valley. Travel through Astak, Jaglot and Gilgit. Stopovers on the way at Rakaposhi viewpoint. Photography and Sightseeing. Arrival in Hunza and transfer to hotel. Visit Karimabad local market.
Overnight stay at Hunza

Breakfast. Departure for Naran. Travel through Babusar Pass and Lulusar Lake with stopovers. Arrival in Naran and transfer to hotel. Free time for adventure activities like rafting or zip-lining, or visit Naran Bazaar.
Overnight stay at Hunza

Breakfast. Whole sightseeing tour to Khunjerab Pass. Visit all majestic locations on the way including Ganish Bridge and Ganish Village, Attabad Lake, Gulmit, Passu Cones, Passu Glacier, Sost and Khunjerab Pass. Drive back to Hunza. Enjoy leisure time in Karimabad local market.
Overnight stay in Hunzas

Breakfast. Drive to Nomal. Transfer to 4x4 and drive to Naltar Valley. Visit majestically beautiful Naltar Valley and Naltar Lakes (Satrangi and Blue Lake). Drive to Nomal and departure for Chilas.
Overnight stay in Chilas

Breakfast. Departure for Islamabad. Travel through same Babusar ‚Äì Naran route. Stopovers on the way. Arrival in Islamabad. End of services.
