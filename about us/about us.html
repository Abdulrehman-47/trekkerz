About Us

Welcome to Trekkerz Travel and Tours, your trusted partner in travel for over 20 years. Established in 2005 and proudly holding a Government-Registered DTS License #9150, Trekkerz has earned its place among the top 5 travel agencies in Pakistan. Specializing in both domestic and international tours, we pride ourselves on reliability, consistency, and budget-friendly services. With over 10,000 satisfied customers, we are dedicated to crafting unforgettable travel experiences that combine professionalism and affordability. Choose Trekkerz for your next journey and let us turn your travel dreams into reality.
